# Ilo: Coastal Charm and Culinary Delights
Ilo, a charming coastal city in the Moquegua region of Peru, is an intriguing destination that offers a unique blend of seaside landscapes, culture, and delicious cuisine. Its scenic boardwalk, with stunning views of the Pacific Ocean, is perfect for enjoying unforgettable sunsets. Visitors can explore Pozo de Lisas Beach, an ideal spot for relaxation, or the Chiribaya Museum, which offers a glimpse into the region's ancient pre-Hispanic cultures. Ilo's gastronomy is another highlight, featuring fresh seafood dishes. Signature meals such as corvina ceviche, shrimp, and the traditional "chupe de camarones" are visitors' must-try delights. Ilo combines natural beauty, history, and irresistible flavors, making it an ideal place to explore.

# How to get to the city of Ilo
## How to get to Ilo from Lima
### Option 1: Lima - Tacna - Ilo
### Airports
The closest airport to Ilo is **Tacna International Airport (TCQ)**, located
approximately 150 km away. The flight from Lima (Jorge Chavez International
Airport) to Tacna takes approximately **1 hour and 50 minutes**. The road trip from
Tacna to Ilo takes between **2 hours and 2 hours 30 minutes**, either by taxi
or bus.

### Transport from Tacna to Ilo
- **Taxi and transportation applications**
Official taxis are available at Tacna airport. The trip to Ilo takes approximately
**2 hours**, with an estimated cost between **$30 and $40**.

- **Bus**
Buses depart from the **Manuel A. Odría Terminal** in Tacna, with an
approximate travel time of **2 hours and 30 minutes** to Ilo. Fares vary between
**$4 and $27**, depending on the transport company.
### Option 2: Lima - Arequipa - Ilo
### Airports
Another option is to fly to **Rodríguez Ballón International Airport (AQP)** in
Arequipa, located **440 km from Ilo**. The flight from Lima to Arequipa takes
approximately **1 hour and 30 minutes**. Then, the road trip from Arequipa to Ilo
takes between **5 and 6 hours**.
### Transportation from Arequipa to Ilo
- **Taxi and transportation applications**
