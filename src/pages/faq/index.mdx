## What is SIMBig?
SIMBig is one of the first conferences in Latin America that groups related areas such as Data Science, Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, Semantic Web, Health Informatics, etc. For more information visit: [simbig.org](https://simbig.org)

## When is the conference?
The important dates in the Event are:
* June 10, 2024: Papers submission deadline
* July 15, 2024: Notification of acceptance
* October 10, 2024: Camera-ready versions due
* October 29 - 31, 2024: Conference held at the Universidad Nacional de Moquegua, Peru

For more information visit: [Important Dates](/#dates)
